History

Rosebank is a neighborhood located on Staten Island. It is bordered by Arrochar to the South and Clifton to the North.

The area was once owned by some of the richest estate families in the country.The Vanderbilts, Aspinwalls and Townsends.They built the first church, St. Johns Church. The first baptism performed in St.Johns Church was Cornilius Vanderbilt, II. His father Donated to Vanderbilt University.

Staten Island is named Richmond County after Duke of Richmond, Charles II's illegitimate son.

The boroughs flag is on a white background in the center is an oval with a blue skyline and 2 black seagulls. The green outline represents countryside, a white outline denotes the residential areas of Staten Island. The words staten Island are inscripted in gold. Below are 5 wavy lines of blue to symbolize the water that surrounds the island borough on all sides.The flag is also surrounded by gold fringes.

Demographics

Recent U.S. census data estimates populations are evenly split among whites (Irish, Italian, Albanians and Russians), African Americans and Hispanics/Latinos each comprising 30% of the total.

Recent immigrants from countries as Sri Lanka, Trinidad, Liberia and the Philippines as well as Mexican have also settled in Central North Shore.
